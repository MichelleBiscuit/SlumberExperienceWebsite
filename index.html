What's the game about?

Tryptamine: Slumber Experience is a game about sleeping and experiencing dreams, whether they are fun, scary, or anything in-between.

What do you do?

You can collect items to display them on your shelf, explore unique worlds never seen before, and see the weird and fantastical creatures of T:SE

Where can I get the full game?

As of right now there is no full game, BUT. You can download the alpha demo here or on itch.io

What engine does this game use?

Godot! The reason why I'm using Godot is because it's free, open source, and very easy and fun to use and mod to my liking.
